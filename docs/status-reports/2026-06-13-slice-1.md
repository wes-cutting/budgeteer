**Resume here:** On top of the Foundation (accounts/envelopes), **Slice 1 is Done and
gate-green**: you can enter a deposit/withdrawal into an account and **allocate** it —
**Single** (whole amount to one envelope) or **Split** (rows with a live `Allocated /
Remaining` tally and a per-row "use remaining"), **partial allowed** ("save now, split
later"), with anything unallocated (including opening balances) surfaced in a
**Needs-allocation** list you can finish from. Account/envelope balances are derived and
update live; over-allocation is rejected client- and server-side. This is the **validated
heart** of the product and it exercises the SPIKE-01 felt-friction caveat in the running
app. **Next: Slice 2 — the accelerators (templates first), then edit-a-past-split.**
