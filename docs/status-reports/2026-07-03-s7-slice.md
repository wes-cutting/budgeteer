float, not the sheet's ~22–27 days; pinning one = the §8 store), ② the `E2E `-fixture cleanup in
the dev store (K24, still pending — the new view makes the pollution very visible: leaked
household-wide targets ≈ $19k/month of planned spending over-commit the demo paycheck).
