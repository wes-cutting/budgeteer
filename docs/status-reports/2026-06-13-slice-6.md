**Resume here:** Envelopes now have a **sinking-fund lifecycle**: archive a finished envelope
(soft-delete) and unarchive it later. Archived envelopes **keep their history and balance**,
**leave the allocation/template pickers**, and the server **blocks new allocations** to them
(the `assertEnvelopesUsable` guard); a Dashboard **Archived** section restores them. No schema
change — `archived_at` has existed since the Foundation. **Next: the remaining domain
capabilities — transfers `#7` (double-entry; likely a small modeling spike first), refunds
`#8`, recurring `#9` — then the analysis area (`#11`–`#14`).**
