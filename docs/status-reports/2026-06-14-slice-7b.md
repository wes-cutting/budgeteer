**Resume here:** Budgeteer now has **both** ADR-0004 money-movement primitives. `#7b` adds
**envelope↔envelope reallocation**: re-budget money between two envelopes (e.g. Groceries →
Vacation) with **no** account movement, via a dedicated `envelope_transfers` row. Envelope
balance is now a **two-source derivation** — `Σ allocations + Σ incoming − Σ outgoing` (the
`v_envelope_balances` view rebuilt). Moving **into** an archived envelope is blocked (`400`),
**draining from** an archived one is allowed, and envelopes may go **negative** (owner decision,
consistent with over-spending). UI: a **Move money between envelopes** form on the Dashboard.
This closes `#7`/ADR-0004 — the two axes are orthogonal and the split model (`#3`–`#6`) is
untouched. **Next: refunds `#8`** (negative allocation within a split), recurring `#9`,
reconcile `#10`, then the **analysis** area (`#11`–`#14`).

Same as #01–#06. New: `POST /envelope-transfers` `{ fromEnvelopeId, toEnvelopeId, amount,
occurredOn?, memo? }` → `201 { envelopeTransfer }`. **Envelope balance is now two-source** —
`v_envelope_balances` = `Σ allocations + Σ incoming − Σ outgoing` envelope transfers; an envelope
transfer creates **no** transaction (accounts untouched). Reallocation into an **archived**
destination is rejected; draining **from** archived is allowed; balances may go **negative**.
