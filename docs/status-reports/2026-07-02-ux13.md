**Resume here:** `UX13` is **done, gate-green**. Budget health was **text-only** — the Budget table
and cockpit budget panel showed target/spent/remaining as bare numbers, and over-budget/negative
money was signalled by a **minus sign alone** (the V1 encoding). This slice adds a **visual** layer on
top of that text: a new **`ProgressBar`** primitive (`apps/web/src/ui/ProgressBar.tsx`) — the last
UX4 seed-and-grow primitive, **hand-rolled on the tokens + CSS with no charting dependency** (the SVG
`Gauge`/`BarChart` from UX8 stay for the Insights charts; utilization/payoff already have a `Gauge`,
so the genuinely new bar surface is budget **target-vs-spent**). Wired at two budget-health surfaces —
a per-row **Spent-of-target** column in `BudgetVsActualView`'s table + a **summary bar** in the
cockpit `BudgetPanel` — plus **weight + colour** on negative money (over-budget remaining ·
liabilities · negative net worth) with the **minus sign kept as the non-colour signal**. A11y: the bar
is **decorative** (`aria-hidden`, no `progressbar` role — no new AT surface, the figure is always
adjacent text); the `over` tone lays a diagonal **hatch SHAPE** over the danger fill; track+fills clear
**3:1** light+dark; **no animation** (reduced-motion-safe). Proven **axe-clean light AND dark** with
both the under- and **over**-budget encoding visible. **No ADR/spike/dep**, no data/API/domain/chart
change. Gate green: **360 Vitest passing** (+6; 1 pre-existing unrelated carry — §4) **+ 91 e2e** (+2),
build **116.87 KB gz** (+0.56 KB, **no dependency**; < 120 KB, ~3.1 KB headroom). **Next:** `UX14`
(empty states & first-run onboarding), then `UX15` (responsive pass).

- **`ProgressBar`** = the inline budget-health bar (`ui/ProgressBar.tsx`). It is **decorative**
  (`aria-hidden`) — always render the truthful figure as adjacent text at the call site; pass `ratio`
  (value ÷ max) + a `tone`. A `ratio > 1` auto-forces the `over` treatment (100% width + hatch shape).
  For a portfolio-scale ratio with a data-table fallback, use the SVG `Gauge` (`ui/Chart.tsx`) instead.
- **Negative money:** encode with **weight + `--color-danger` tone AND the minus sign** (never colour
  alone). In the cockpit, pass `negative: true` to a `Figures` item; in the Budget table, the
  `overText` class does it.
- **Bundle:** 120 KB gz ceiling, 116.87 KB now (~3.1 KB headroom). Measure any new dependency against
  this tight margin — a second new dep would likely breach.
- **Known carries (not this slice's regressions):** `recurring.test.ts`'s post-due occurrence-count
  case, and a flaky `getByLabel("Account")` collision in the forecast e2e — both reproduce on `main`,
  see §4.
