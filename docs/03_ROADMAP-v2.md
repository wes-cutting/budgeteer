## 1. The id convention

- **Type-tagged, project-prefixed, one counter per type that never restarts:** `BUD-E##`
  (epic), `BUD-S##` (story), `BUD-T##` (task). The number is a **stable handle, not a
  position** — re-sequencing never renames. Order lives in the document layout + the status
  column, never in the id.
- **Hierarchy two ways:** the type tag says *what* an item is; the epic grouping + a `Parent`
  reading (each story sits under its epic heading) says *where it rolls up*.
- **Spikes keep `SPIKE-##`** (already consistent) and are listed under the epic they de-risked.
- Legacy ids are preserved in every table's **`Was`** column and in §2.
